Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the Univeristy of Michigan. I am currently visiting the Computational Vision and Geometry Lab at Stanford University, under the supervision of Professor Silvio Savarese. My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering: Systems from the Univeristy of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, in 2013, and my B.S. degree in Electronics Engineering from National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan, in 2009. From 2010 to 2011, I worked as a research assistant at the Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan, where I was advised by Dr. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang.
