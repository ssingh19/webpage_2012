Biography

I am a second-year M.S. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Univeristy of Michigan. Currently I am a member of the Vision Lab, under the supervision of Professor Silvio Savarese. My research interests are in computer vision, pattern recognition, and machine learning. I received my B.S. degree in Electronics Engineering from National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan, in 2009. In my senior year, I was an exchange student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. From 2010 to 2011, I worked as a research assistant at the Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan, where I was advised by Dr. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang.
